Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common queries about our services

1. What services do you offer?

We provide household shifting, office relocation, car transportation, and packing services across India.

2. Do you provide packing materials?

Yes, we provide all necessary materials like cartons, bubble wrap, foam, and stretch film for safe packing.

3. How much time before should I book?

We recommend booking at least 2-3 days in advance, but we also handle urgent shifts depending on availability.

4. How do I track my order?

Use our Track Order page and enter your consignment number to view the status.

5. Is there insurance for items?

Yes, insurance is available upon request for valuable or fragile items.
